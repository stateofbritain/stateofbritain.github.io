# Data Not Published

User questions that identify data the UK government does not publish (or publishes in a form too limited to be useful). Each entry is verified before being added here.

This file serves two purposes:
1. Records genuine data gaps in UK government transparency
2. Prevents repeated research into data that doesn't exist

## Confirmed gaps

### Welfare spending by nationality/country of birth of recipient
- **Requested**: "How much of the welfare budget goes to foreign born residents?"
- **Verified**: 2026-04-01
- **Detail**: DWP published an experimental series "Nationality at point of NINo registration of DWP benefit recipients" but discontinued it in August 2022. The last data covered November 2020. DWP stated nationality at NINo registration increasingly diverged from current nationality (many EU nationals acquired UK citizenship post-Brexit). The series only covered working-age benefit caseloads, never expenditure. No current official publication breaks down benefit spending or caseloads by nationality or country of birth.
- **Partial alternatives**: Family Resources Survey has some benefit receipt data by ethnicity (not nationality). Migration Advisory Committee reports contain estimates.

## Investigated but found to be published

### Crime statistics by ethnicity
- **Requested**: "What ethnic groups commit the most crime per capita"
- **Verified**: 2026-04-01
- **Finding**: MoJ publishes "Ethnicity and the Criminal Justice System" annually (latest: 2024 edition, published Nov 2025). Covers stop and search, arrests, prosecutions, convictions, sentencing, prison population by five broad ethnic groups (Asian, Black, Mixed, White, Other). Includes some per-capita rates (e.g. homicide victimisation per million). Raw counts in ODS tables can be combined with Census population denominators to calculate additional per-capita rates. Data covers England and Wales.
- **Source**: https://www.gov.uk/government/statistics/ethnicity-and-the-criminal-justice-system-2024
